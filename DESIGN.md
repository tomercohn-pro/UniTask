# UniTask Design System

## Colors
- **Primary:** `#4F46E5` (Indigo - Buttons, Active icons)
- **Secondary:** `#818CF8` (Light Indigo - Hover states)
- **Accent:** `#F59E0B` (Amber - Urgent tags)
- **Background:** `#F9FAFB` (Off-white - Main background)
- **Text (Main):** `#111827` (Dark Gray - Headings, Body)
- **Text (Muted):** `#6B7280` (Gray - Secondary text, Inactive icons)
- **Error:** `#EF4444` (Red)
- **Success:** `#10B981` (Emerald)

## Typography
- **Font Family:** 'Heebo', sans-serif
- **Headings (H1/H2):** 24px, Bold (700)
- **Body:** 16px, Regular (400)
- **Caption/Labels:** 14px, Medium (500)

## Spacing (Base Unit: 8px)
- Small: 8px
- Medium: 16px
- Large: 24px
- X-Large: 32px

## Border Radius
- Cards/Containers: 16px
- Buttons/Inputs: 8px
